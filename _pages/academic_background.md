## Publications

<a href="https://doi.org/10.1103/mj3d-yq87" target="_blank"><b>High-precision continuum limit study of the HVP short-distance window</b></a> <br>
Authors: **Sebastian Spiegel**, Christoph Lehner <br>
Journal: *Physical Review D 111, 114517* (2025) <br>

This work contributed to the <a href="https://doi.org/10.48550/arXiv.2505.21476" target="_blank">2025 Muon g-2 Theory Initiative White Paper</a>.



Using supercomputer simulations, we performed the first high-precision, first-principles lattice QCD calculation of the hadronic vacuum polarization (HVP) short-distance window contribution to the muon anomalous magnetic moment $a_\mu = (g-2)/2$. The result directly enters the current Standard Model prediction of $a_\mu$, a key benchmark for precision tests of fundamental particle physics.
